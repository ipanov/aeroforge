### 3.4 H-stab to Wing Area Ratio Summary

Across all measured models, the H-stab area is **8.9-11.6% of wing area**, with a median of approximately **10%**. This is smaller than the typical 20-25% ratio for powered aircraft, consistent with sailplane design practice where:
- Lower flight speeds reduce stability requirements
- Longer tail moment arms compensate for smaller tail area
- Minimizing tail area reduces wetted area drag
