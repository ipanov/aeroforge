# Wing_Half_Assembly

## Purpose

Canonical half-wing assembly parent. This is the missing top-down layer that sits above
the panel components and below the full aircraft integration.

## Workflow Position

This assembly drawing must be treated as the source artifact for:

- panel boundaries
- spar routing through the half-wing
- hinge/control zoning
- polyhedral break placement
- mass rollup at the half-wing level

## Symmetry Rule

This folder defines one half-wing only. The opposite side is mirrored at aircraft assembly time.
