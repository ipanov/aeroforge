# Component: HStab

## Description

Horizontal stabilizer half-shell (symmetric — print 2, mirror one for opposite side), forming the fixed lifting surface of the empennage. The shell uses a superellipse planform (n=2.3) with airfoil blending from HT-13 at the root to HT-12 at the tip, printed in vase mode from LW-PLA. Features include a 3.1mm spar bore at X=34.5mm (30% chord), a concealed saddle hinge zone at X=60mm with reinforced wall thickness, and a 0.6mm music wire bore for the concealed piano-wire hinge system.

## Assembly Notes

- The 3mm carbon spar tube slides through the 3.1mm bore at X=34.5mm, passing through both left and right halves and bonding at the root joint.
- The hinge saddle at X=60mm mates with the elevator bull-nose via alternating PETG sleeves threaded on the 0.5mm music wire.
- Root face bonds to the right half at the Y=0 centerline. Alignment is set by the spar tube.
- The trailing edge aft of X=60mm is the elevator cutline; the stab shell stops at the hinge face.
