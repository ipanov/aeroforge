# Component: Hinge_Wire

## Description
The continuous pin hinge axis for the elevator. A single straight length of music wire (ASTM A228 spring steel) that threads through all PETG hinge knuckle bores along the elevator–stab junction. The 90° bent ends prevent axial pull-out. Music wire is chosen for its hardness (Rockwell C55+), corrosion resistance, and tight dimensional tolerance — the 0.5mm diameter is a close slip fit in the knuckle bores.
