# Component: Mass_Balance

## Description
A small blob of tungsten putty applied to the forward extension of the elevator control horn. By adding mass ahead of the hinge line, it moves the elevator's center of mass forward, significantly raising the flutter speed. Tungsten putty is the standard RC/sailplane approach: it is dense (~17 g/cc), non-toxic, easily shaped, and sticks to plastic without adhesive. The mass is tuned during final balance — typically 0.8–1.2g achieves a near-neutral or slightly nose-heavy elevator balance.

- Special notes: Roll into a small ball and press onto the control horn forward arm after the elevator is completed. Adjust mass until the elevator balance point (about hinge line) is neutral or 2–3mm nose heavy. Do NOT use lead putty — tungsten is 2× denser so the blob is smaller and does not affect aerodynamics.
