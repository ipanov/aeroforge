# Component: Elevator_Left

## Description

Left elevator half with a concealed bull-nose hinge, forming the movable control surface of the horizontal stabilizer. The elevator chord is approximately 45% of the total stab chord at the root (51.5mm), with the hinge line at X=60mm. The bull-nose extends 2.5mm forward of the hinge line at the root and tapers to zero at y=206mm, providing aerodynamic sealing and a smooth hinge gap. A 0.6mm wire bore carries the concealed piano-wire hinge, and a 1.6mm pushrod hole at X=70mm accepts the control linkage.

## Assembly Notes

- The bull-nose interlocks with the hinge saddle on HStab_Left, with alternating PETG sleeves on the 0.5mm music wire providing the bearing surfaces.
- The elevator starts at the hinge face; cross-sections of this component show only the elevator shell, not the stab airfoil forward of the hinge.
- Pushrod connects at X=70mm through the 1.6mm hole to a micro servo horn below.
- Deflection range: approximately +/- 25 degrees, limited by the bull-nose/saddle clearance geometry.
- Trailing edge is sharp and unsupported; handle with care during assembly.
