# Component: HStab_Right

## Description

Right horizontal stabilizer half-shell, a mirror of HStab_Left across the Y=0 centerline. Identical geometry, airfoil blending, spar bore, hinge saddle, and wall thicknesses as the left half. Printed in vase mode from LW-PLA with the saddle face down.

## Assembly Notes

- Mirror image of HStab_Left. The STL can be generated by mirroring the left half across Y=0, or by running the parametric script with `mirror=True`.
- The 3mm carbon spar tube passes through both halves continuously. Bond at the root joint with CA or epoxy.
- Hinge saddle and wire bore are mirrored and align with Elevator_Right.
- Root face bonds to the left half at the Y=0 centerline.
