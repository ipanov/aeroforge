# Component: Hinge_Wire

## Description

Single length of 0.5mm music wire (spring steel, ASTM A228) serving as the concealed piano-wire hinge for the elevator. The wire runs the full span of the stabilizer along the hinge line at X=60.0mm, passing through alternating PETG sleeves that are fixed to the stab and elevator shells respectively. Each tip has a 90-degree bend with an 8mm retention leg to prevent axial migration.

## Assembly Notes

- Thread the wire through all 48 PETG sleeves before inserting into the stab/elevator assembly. Alternating sleeves are bonded to the stab shell (fixed) and elevator shell (rotating).
- The 90-degree bends at each tip retain the wire axially. Bend after threading using needle-nose pliers.
- The wire must be straight and free of kinks; any kink will bind the hinge. Roll on a flat surface to verify straightness before assembly.
- Source: music wire is available from any hobby shop or online supplier. Common packaging is 1m lengths; cut to 424mm and bend tips.
- Do not substitute with softer wire (craft wire, florist wire). Music wire spring temper is required for fatigue life and low friction.
