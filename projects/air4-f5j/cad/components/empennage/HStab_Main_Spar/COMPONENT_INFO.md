# Component: HStab_Main_Spar

## Description

Single carbon fiber tube serving as the main structural spar for the horizontal stabilizer. The tube passes continuously through both left and right stab halves at the 30% chord line (X=34.5mm), providing bending stiffness and alignment. Pultruded carbon fiber tube, sourced off-the-shelf from any RC hobby supplier.

## Assembly Notes

- The spar slides through the 3.1mm bores in HStab_Left and HStab_Right, spanning the full 430mm stabilizer span (215mm per half) with the tube trimmed to 378mm to sit slightly inboard of the tips.
- Bond with medium CA or 5-minute epoxy at the root joint and at each tip bore exit.
- The spar is the primary alignment datum during assembly: insert spar first, then bond root faces.
- Do not sand or nick the carbon tube surface; pultruded CF is brittle and surface damage initiates splitting.
- Source: any RC hobby supplier selling 3mm OD / 2mm ID pultruded CF tubes. Common lengths are 500mm or 1000mm; cut to 378mm with a rotary tool and fine diamond disc.
