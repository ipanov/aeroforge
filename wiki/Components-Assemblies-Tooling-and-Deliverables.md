[docs/framework/components-assemblies-tooling-and-deliverables.md](https://github.com/ipanov/aeroforge/blob/master/docs/framework/components-assemblies-tooling-and-deliverables.md)

- Components are the lowest tracked parts.
- Assemblies combine components and/or lower assemblies.
- Tooling and manufacturing technique are project decisions.
- Deliverables vary by node and by project type.
